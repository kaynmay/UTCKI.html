About Us

Welcome to the UT Chapter of Circle K International! Sponsored by Kiwanis International, CKI transforms university students into a global network of responsible citizens and leaders with a lifelong commitment to service. In layman's terms, we make service fun by helping you build lifelong relationships and learn how to be a terrific leader!

Our meetings are held Tuesdays at 7:00 pm in room PHR 2.114 on University Avenue. These meetings include learning about service and social opportunities, meeting with tight-knit committees, completing simple and fun service projects, and getting to know great people! There are more than 13,770 CKI members in 17 different nations. If you wish to make a positive impact on the world join us today!
