Welcome to the UT Chapter of Circle K International! CKI transforms university students into a global network of responsible citizens with a lifelong commitment to service. We make service fun by helping you build lifelong relationships and by teaching you how to become a terrific leader. Our meetings are held on Tuesdays at 7:00 pm in room PHR 2.114 on University Avenue. If you're interested in joining, please feel free to check out our About Us page for more information on the chapter and how to get involved. If you're already a member, make sure to log your service hours and stay up to date with any upcoming projects.
